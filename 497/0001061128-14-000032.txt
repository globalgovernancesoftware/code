<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Portfolio Turnover</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Fund pays transaction costs, such as commissions, when it buys and sells securities (or &#8220;turns over&#8221; its portfolio).&#160;&#160;A higher portfolio turnover rate may indicate higher transaction costs.&#160;&#160;These costs, which are not reflected in annual fund operating expenses or in the example, affect the Fund&#8217;s performance.&#160;&#160;During the most recent fiscal year, the Fund&#8217;s portfolio turnover rate was 66.24%<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">&#160;</font>of the average value of its portfolio.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 15.95pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 12pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold"><a name="InvestStrategyRisks"><!--EFPlaceholder--></a>Investment Strategy, Risks and Performance</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 15.95pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Principal Investment Strategy</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Fund has a &#8220;dual investment strategy&#8221; and invests primarily in common stocks of U.S. companies, the majority of which are income producing.&#160;&#160;The Fund also may invest in American Depository Receipts, which represent shares of stock of foreign companies.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Fund has four sub-advisors that independently manage a portion of the Fund&#8217;s portfolio using different investment strategies.&#160;&#160;The approach of two of the sub-advisors (Boston Advisors, LLC (&#8220;Boston Advisors&#8221;) and The Renaissance Group LLC (&#8220;Renaissance&#8221;)) is growth oriented and generally focuses on purchasing stock of companies that exhibit above average earnings and capital growth.&#160;&#160;The other two sub-advisors (Beck, Mack &amp; Oliver LLC (&#8220;BM&amp;O&#8221;) and WEDGE Capital Management L.L.P. (&#8220;Wedge&#8221;)) are &#8220;value&#8221; sub-advisors, generally focusing on undervalued equity securities with large capitalizations.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">To facilitate the Fund achieving its investment objectives, the Fund retains sub-advisors with different principal investment strategies.&#160;&#160;Each of the Fund&#8217;s sub-advisors follows its own particular strategy with regard to the portion of the Fund&#8217;s assets under its management, as described below.&#160;&#160;In addition, the sub-advisors are contractually obligated to adhere to the Fund&#8217;s fundamental and non-fundamental investment policies.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">Growth Sub-Advisors</font></font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Boston Advisors&#8217; primary objective is to generate growth of capital, and it invests in stocks of companies with a market capitalization of greater than $1 billion and earnings expected to grow faster than the market average.&#160;&#160;Boston Advisors uses a proprietary process to compare and rank companies, with the primary objective of generating growth of capital.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Renaissance focuses on stocks that have and are expected to continue experiencing above average earnings growth.&#160;&#160;Using the Renaissance financial models, Renaissance&#8217;s buy discipline requires (1) an analysis of the profitability and financial strength of a company, (2) scoring and ranking of other factors, and (3) overall assessment of the company and its opportunity for growth.&#160;&#160;Renaissance&#8217;s sell discipline is a function of its quantitative scoring and ranking process, and any security falling below the 40<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">th</font> percentile is reviewed for sale to be potentially replaced with a more highly ranked security chosen from the top 20<font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-SIZE: 70%; VERTICAL-ALIGN: text-top">th</font> percentile of the strategy&#8217;s universe.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; TEXT-DECORATION: underline">Value Sub-Advisors</font></font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">BM&amp;O relies primarily on fundamental analysis to identify companies that, in its judgment, are undervalued, financially strong and possess high quality assets and above average appreciation potential.&#160;&#160;In this manner, BM&amp;O aims to purchase portfolio securities at low price levels relative to earnings and valuations.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt"><font id="TAB1" style="MARGIN-LEFT: 36pt"></font>WEDGE employs a series of models to analyze the largest 1,000 companies traded in the U.S. and identify undervalued stocks that are beginning to show visible signs of life.&#160;&#160;All buy, hold and sell decisions are reviewed for accuracy of data and current developments that might alter the quantitative analysis, but WEDGE will not over-ride the model with subjective judgment.</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify">&#160;</div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 0pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="left"><font style="FONT-STYLE: italic; DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Principal Risks of Investing</font></div>

<div style="LINE-HEIGHT: 11.4pt; TEXT-INDENT: 36pt; DISPLAY: block; MARGIN-LEFT: 0pt; MARGIN-RIGHT: 0pt" align="justify"><font style="DISPLAY: inline; FONT-FAMILY: Times New Roman; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Depending on changes in the value of the Fund&#8217;s investments, the Fund&#8217;s shares will rise and fall in value, affecting the Fund&#8217;s total return to investors.&#160;&#160;You may lose money on your investment in the Fund.&#160;&#160;Below is a summary of the principal risks of investing in the Fund, including the risks to which the Fund&#8217;s portfolio as a whole is subject and the circumstances reasonably likely to adversely affect the Fund&#8217;s net asset value and total return.</font></div>
